About TravelBuddy AI

The most intelligent AI travel planner built for Indian travellers — powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash, completely free, no login required.

Our Mission

Travel planning in India is broken — overpriced agents, generic recommendations, outdated info. Indian travellers deserve better. TravelBuddy AI was built to change that.

We use Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash to create perfectly optimised itineraries matching your exact budget, interests and travel style. From ₹10,000 Goa trips to ₹5 lakh Europe tours — planned in 30 seconds. No login. No credit card. No nonsense.
